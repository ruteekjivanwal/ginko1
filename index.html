About us

The only thing we’re serious about is food.

Ginkgo is a brand that embodies everything for everyone. The perfect amalgamation of fun, quirky, cheeky and wholesome, its a one-stop-shop for all things Asian. If its date night, family movie night, or even just a meal for one at the office, Ginkgo’s got you covered for it all.
